Welcome to the LoneVibes! Here's a quick guide to help you navigate the app:

How to Use the App

      1️⃣Search for music: Use the search bar at the top of the main page to find songs in the music list.
      2️⃣Play a song: Click on any song title to play it in the audio player.
      3️⃣Add to Favorites: Click the heart icon next to a song to add it to your Favorites. You can view all your favorites on the Favorites Page.
      4️⃣Add to Playlist: Click the plus icon next to a song to add it to your Playlist. You can view your playlist on the My Playlist Page.
      5️⃣View and manage Favorites & Playlist: Go to the Favorites Page or My Playlist Page to view, remove, or play songs you added.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, feel free to reach out!
